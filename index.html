Finally a user-friendly solution for Kubernetes backups!

Backup your precious data at whatever intervals you want! Bnkr allows you to choose from hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or you can use your cron! With more than 20 themes, manual backup and restore, timezone per backup, multiple recipients, and much more features that make Bnkr the best fit for the backup job!

Do you want to migrate your data? no problem!

Migrate your precious data in a bidirectional way between Kubernetes, Servers, and S3! With Bnkr migration feature you can migrate files, MySQL/MariaDB databases, PostgreSQL databases, and MongoDB databases between Kubernetes, Servers, and S3 in a bidirectional way!
